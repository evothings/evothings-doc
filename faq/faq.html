Why can't my app connect to my server?

Evothings applications running in the viewer are served over HTTPS from our proxy servers in the cloud. This means any connections made from the application must be secure. Your application must use HTTPS or WSS (Secure Web Sockets) with the server presenting proper (not self signed) certificates.
